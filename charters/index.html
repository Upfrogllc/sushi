Fish with a Champion

Charter the Sushi

Fish aboard the only boat to ever win both the White Marlin Open and Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament. Captain Charley Pereira brings over 25 years of experience, world-class equipment, and a faith-driven approach to every trip. All tackle is included. Families welcome.

Full Day Offshore

A full day on the Gulf Stream aboard the Sushi, targeting blue marlin, white marlin, yellowfin tuna, wahoo, and mahi-mahi. The Sushi's 55 mph cruising speed gets you to the fishing grounds before anyone else. Captain Charley reads the water, the currents, and the temperature breaks — the same approach that's produced $5.9 million in tournament winnings.

8–12 Hours · Up to 6 Anglers · All Tackle Included

Tournament Prep

Train with a two-time major champion. Charley walks your team through rigging, the physics of keeping hooks in mouths, fighting technique, boat positioning, and tournament strategy. Full dock drills included. This is the same preparation process that produced the WMO and Big Rock wins.

Full or Half Day · Dock Drills · Strategy Session

Tuna & Bottom Fishing

Yellowfin tuna, bluefin tuna, tilefish, grouper, snapper, cobia, and more. The Sushi's speed and range open up fishing grounds that most charter boats can't reach in a day trip. 820 gallons of fuel and 55 mph means you'll be first to the deep water and home before dark.

6–10 Hours · All Tackle · Up to 6 Anglers
